About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive roguelike survival game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Choose your character, level up unique abilities, and see how long you can survive against the monster hordes.

With its pixel-perfect art style and engaging gameplay mechanics, Monster Survivors offers a challenging experience that will keep you coming back for "just one more run."

The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two playthroughs are ever exactly the same. Adapt your strategy on the fly as you encounter different monster types and collect powerful upgrades.

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from various characters, each with unique abilities and playstyles.

Weapon Evolution

Combine weapons and items to create powerful evolved versions with special effects.

Roguelike Elements

Every run is different with random map generation and item drops.

Progression System

Level up during runs and unlock permanent upgrades for future attempts.
